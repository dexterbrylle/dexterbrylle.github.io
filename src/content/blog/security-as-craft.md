---
title: "Security as Craft"
description: "A working note on treating security as part of engineering practice."
pubDate: 2026-05-06
tags: ["security", "engineering"]
---

Security works best when it is part of the build habit, not an inspection at
the end. Good defaults, small surfaces, clear ownership, and routine review do
more than dramatic last-minute hardening.

The goal is not to make systems ornate. The goal is to make them understandable
enough that mistakes are easier to see.
